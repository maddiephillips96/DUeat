Quick meals shouldn't always be pot noodle or a peice of toast! This recipe ensures that you don't compensate on the taste of your food!

The perfect basic burger


The burger is a classic dish that doesn't just have to be ordered in restaurants. You can serve it with a classic bun or if you want to be super healthy you can substitute this for two flat mushrooms or lettuce leaves!! This recipe only takes around 15 minutes and is perfect for when you are in a hurry!


Ingredients
  • 1 egg

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 pound of ground beef

  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs


Method
  1. Pre-heat the grill

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg, salt and pepper. Place ground beef and bread crumbs into the mixture. With hands or a fork, mix until well blended

  3. Form into 4 burgers approximately 3/4 inch thick.

  4. Place the burgers on the grill and cover and cook for 6 to 8 minutes per side.

  5. Serve with preferred fillings
