Mocktails are increasing in popularity and are a great way to enjoy social settings without feeling that you're missing out and more importantly, they are absolutely delicious.

Creating alcohol-free alternatives to cocktails is easy and so much fun too. We have a bunch of recipes below to choose from and we walk you through how best to prepare them.

The Famous No-jito aka Mojito


The Nojito is a mocktail version of the Mojito, which is just as tasty as the original. All that refreshing mint and lime flavor remains, you just lose the alcohol. The rum is replaced with club soda and a bit of brown sugar which makes up for the hint of sweetness that rum adds. You may want to use Sprite or 7-Up instead of club soda if you like the drink a bit sweeter.

Ingredients
  • 12 to 14 small mint leaves or 6-8 big ones

  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

  • 4 ounces club soda (Sprite works, too)


Method
  1. Put the mint leaves, lime juice and brown sugar in a tall cocktail glass

  2. Muddle the leaves

  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes

  4. Add the club soda

  5. Stir to mix up the sugar

  6. Garnish with another mint sprig
